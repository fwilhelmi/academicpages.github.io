**Abstract:** 
The talk is intended to present, describe and solve doubts concerning the problem statement “Improving the capacity of IEEE 802.11 WLANs through Machine Learning” [https://www.upf.edu/web/wnrg/ai_challenge](https://www.upf.edu/web/wnrg/ai_challenge) of the ITU AI/ML in 5G Challenge. The first part of the talk will be devoted to introducing IEEE 802.11 WLANs and the Channel Bonding (CB) problem. Then, the dataset will be described together with the goals of the challenge.

The presentation can be watched at [https://aiforgood.itu.int/events/itu-ai-ml-in-5g-challenge-improving-the-capacity-of-ieee-802-11-wlans-through-machine-learning/](https://aiforgood.itu.int/events/itu-ai-ml-in-5g-challenge-improving-the-capacity-of-ieee-802-11-wlans-through-machine-learning/).
